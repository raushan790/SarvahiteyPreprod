Who we are

SARVAHITEY is a non-profit organization made up of cause-based members and volunteers striving to preserve and sustain our community.

Founded in 2014 SARVAHITEY aims to address the many issues of the India's growing rural-urban population.

We are a movement of Young people taking ownership of empowering the marginalized of our community.
